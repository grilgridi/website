A Random Race
The game selects a Grand Prix from Formula 1 history at random. You will not know which race it is until you press Play. Races range from Easy to Hard depending on how well-known the result is.
🧩
Fill the Grid
Some finishing positions are hidden and shown as empty slots. The drivers you need to place appear as chips at the top of the screen. Drag a chip onto a slot, or tap a chip first and then tap the slot you want to fill it with.
✅
Check Your Answers
Once all slots are filled, press Check. Correct answers turn green and stay in place. Wrong answers are removed and you lose one life. You have 3 lives per race. Lose all three and the game ends.
❤️
Lives
You start each race with 3 lives. Every time you press Check and have at least one wrong answer, you lose a life. When you run out of lives the game is over, so think carefully before checking.
💡
Hints
You start with 1 free hint per race. Press the Hint button, then tap the slot you want revealed. The correct driver will be placed automatically. Watch a short ad to earn one extra hint if you need it. Using hints reduces your potential score.
